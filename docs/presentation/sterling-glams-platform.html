The big idea

One platform. One database. No silos.

Every channel reads and writes the same live data, so stock, sales, customers and prices are always in sync — in the shop and online, at the same time.

  • Customer storefront — browse, search, buy, track orders, wishlist, loyalty.
  • Point of Sale — a fast in-store till for all three boutiques (Abuja, Allen, Ikota).
  • Inventory system — real stock across branches, transfers, stock-takes, ledgers.
  • Marketing hub — campaigns, automations, abandoned-cart recovery, referrals.
  • Admin backend — products, orders, customers, reports, settings, staff & roles.

Replaced the previous third‑party ERP — the website database is now the single source of truth, owned by us, with no per‑seat licence fees.

Payments

Taking money, safely

  • We never store card details. Customers pay on the provider’s secure hosted page.
  • Webhooks are cryptographically verified (HMAC signature) — a fake “paid” message is rejected.
  • Amounts are re‑checked — an order is only fulfilled if the money actually covers the total.
  • Provider is switchable and keys are entered in the admin, encrypted at rest, visible to the owner only.
  • Refunds are handled in‑system with a full audit trail and stock return.
Inventory system

Real stock, every branch

  • Per‑variant, per‑store balances — know exactly what’s where.
  • Movement ledger — every change (sale, return, transfer, adjustment) is recorded and typed.
  • Branch transfers with approve → dispatch → receive workflow.
  • Stock‑takes with barcode scanning and variance reporting.
  • Low‑stock alerts and reports for reordering decisions.
  • Store‑level access control — staff only touch the branches they’re assigned to.
  • Barcode‑native — scan to find, count, or sell.
  • Reserved vs available — online holds never oversell the shop floor.
  • Full audit — who changed what, when, and why.
The differentiator

One stock pool — online and in‑store

The website and every till draw from the same live ledger. Sell the last bracelet at the Allen counter and it disappears from the website instantly — and vice‑versa.

✓ No overselling

Checkout locks the stock row and re‑checks under load — two sales can’t both take the last piece.

✓ Always accurate

No nightly sync, no spreadsheets — the number you see is the truth.

✓ One customer view

Loyalty, history and profiles are shared across web and till.

Security · in depth

Every layer, hardened

  • HTTPS everywhere and a strict Content‑Security‑Policy to block injection.
  • Anti‑forgery tokens on every action that changes data.
  • Rate‑limiting on login & PIN entry to stop brute‑force.
  • Hidden staff URLs — admin areas live behind configurable secret paths.
  • Concurrency‑safe money — sales & refunds lock rows so nothing double‑counts.
  • Idempotent operations — a repeated webhook or offline re‑sync never charges or deducts twice.
  • Full audit log — a permanent record of who did what, with before/after values.
  • Owner‑only integrations — payment & email keys are visible to you alone.
